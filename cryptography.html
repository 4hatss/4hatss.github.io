CyberChef

A powerful online “data manipulation” tool used to encode, decode, convert, analyze, and transform data. Very useful in CTFs for handling Base64, hex, XOR, hashing, compression, and more.

dCode

A tool that tries to detect what type of cipher or encryption was used. You paste encrypted text, and it guesses if it's Caesar, Vigenère, substitution, or another classical cipher.

FactorDB

A large online database of numbers and their prime factors. Very useful in RSA CTF problems to check if the modulus n has known factors or weaknesses.

Go to Resource »

Online Hash Crack

A website that identifies what type of hash you have (MD5, SHA-1, SHA-256, NTLM, etc.). Useful before attempting to crack a hash in CTF challenges
